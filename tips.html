Business Tips for Entrepreneurs

Starting a shoe business in Lesotho? Here are some key tips to help you grow:

  • Identify your target market and cater to their specific needs.
  • Focus on quality and comfort to build a loyal customer base.
  • Use social media platforms to showcase your products and engage with customers.
  • Invest in good suppliers to ensure a reliable stock of shoes.
  • Offer excellent customer service to stand out in the competitive market.
